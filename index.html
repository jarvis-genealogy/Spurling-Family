Spurling family

The Spurling family in Port Augusta, South Australia


The Spurling family is linked to Jarvis family through Shirley Sanderson who married Keith Jarvis of Adelaide, South Australia. Shirley's mother was Gertrude Spurling who married Verner Stuart Leslie Sanderson of Port Augusta.



The Spurling family tree

Click on the link above to open the Spurling family tree. This gives an overview of the family and relationships. If there are blue squares at the left margin, move the cursor over them to bring up thumbnail images. Click on the square to bring up the particular image or document.

A "tree" icon leads to a separate family tree for that person, in this case the Sanderson Family for Gertrude Spurling, the author's grandmother who married Verner Stuart Leslie Sanderson.

Where there are further details for a person, the name will be highlighted, Click on the name to bring up a profile page. References may be listed where more information is available. Click the "Back" button at the top of the browser to return to the chart.
